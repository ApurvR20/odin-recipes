Paneer Bhurji Sandwich

Description

Paneer bhurji sandwich is a delicious Indian sandwich that is made with a spicy and flavorful filling of scrambled paneer, onions, tomatoes, and a variety of spices. Paneer, which is a type of Indian cheese (Cottage Cheese), is the star ingredient in this dish and is loved for its soft and creamy texture. The filling is typically made by cooking crumbled paneer with onions, tomatoes, green chilies, and a blend of spices such as cumin, coriander, and turmeric.

Ingredients

  • For Paneer Bhurji

    • 200 Grams Paneer (Cottage Cheese), crumbled
    • 1 tbsp Oil
    • 1/2 tbsp Cumin seeds
    • A pinch of asafoetida
    • 1 tbsp Cumin seeds
    • 1 tsp Fennel seeds
    • Salt to taste
    • 1/4 tbsp turmeric powder
    • 1/4 tsp Garam Masala Powder
    • 1 tbsp Coriander Leaves

  • For Sandwich

    • 8 to 10 slices of Brown Bread or White Bread
    • Butter

Steps

  • For Paneer Bhurji

    1. Heat a pan, add oil, butter in it then add cumin seeds saute on a low flame till they crackle
    2. Now add ginger saute ginger for a minute on low flame, till the raw smell vanishes from the ginger.
    3. Add onions and green chilies and saute till onion becomes translucent.
    4. Sprinkle salt as per taste.
    5. Add tomato stir and saute for a minute. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder and garam masala mix well.
    6. Saute the tomatoes for 2 to 3 minutes, until the tomatoes soften.
    7. Add the crumbled paneer, stir and mix well. Cook for 1 to 2 mins on low heat.
    8. Switch off the flame and add chopped coriander leaves. Stir all nicely.
    9. Remove the paneer bhurji in a bowl and place aside. Allow it to cool at room temperature.

  • Making paneer bhurji toast sandwiches

    1. Apply a generous amount of butter to the bread.
    2. Add a spoonful of paneer bhurji mixture on the buttered bread. Spread mixture evenly on all sides with the help of a spatula or back of a spoon.
    3. Cover with another slice of bread.
    4. Place the sandwiches in a grill pan and grilled for 2 to 3 mins or until they look golden brown and crisp. Similarly grill from both sides and rest of sandwiches.
    5. Slice the sandwiches, apply some butter over them. Serve paneer bhurji sandwich with green chutney and tomato ketchup.
