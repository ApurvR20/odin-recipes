Masala Soda

Description

Masala soda is a popular Indian beverage that is made by mixing soda water with a variety of spices and herbs. This refreshing and fizzy drink is a staple at street food stalls and restaurants all over India, and is especially popular during the hot summer months. The combination of soda water and spices makes for a unique taste that is both sweet and tangy, and is sure to tantalize your taste buds. Masala soda is also known for its digestive properties, as the spices used in its preparation can help to soothe an upset stomach and aid in digestion.

Ingredients

  • For Masala

    • 1 tbsp Dry Mango powder
    • 1/2 tbsp Black salt
    • 1/2 tbsp Black salt
    • A pinch of asafoetida
    • 1 tbsp Cumin seeds
    • 1 tsp Fennel seeds
    • Salt to taste
    • 1/4 cup Sugar

  • For Masala Soda

    • Fresh Mint leaves
    • 1/2 spoon Lime Juice
    • 1 tbsp Prepared Masala
    • Soda
    • 1 tbsp Basil seeds

  • Other Ingredients

    • Few Boondi
    • Few Ice cubes

Steps

  • For Masala

    1. In a bowl, add dry mango powder, black salt, asafoetida
    2. In a pan, add cumin seeds, fennel seeds, black peppercorns dry roast on medium flame.
    3. Transfer this into a bowl, add salt to taste and sugar.
    4. Transfer this into a grinder jar and grind it into a fine powder.
    5. Keep in an airtight container for further use.

  • For Masala Soda

    1. In a serving glass, add some mint leaves, prepared masala and a few ice cubes.
    2. Just before serving, pour the soda over it, garnish it with soaked basil seeds.
    3. Served chilled.
